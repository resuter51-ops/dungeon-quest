SALVAGER'S QUEST

A Roguelike Dungeon Crawler

How to Play

• Use arrow keys / WASD or the D-pad to move
• Move into enemies to attack them
• Find the stairs ▶ to descend deeper
• Collect gold, potions, and gear
• Level up to choose powerful upgrades
• Visit the Merchant 🏪 to buy items
• Find the Amulet on Floor 20 to win!
• Death is permanent — choose wisely

Progress auto-saves locally.


CHOOSE YOUR PATH

The dungeon doesn't care who you are. How hard do you want it to?

ADVENTURER NORMAL
Balanced combat with fair odds. Recommended for first-time delvers.
• Standard enemy stats • Normal loot drops • Full healing potions
WARRIOR HARD
Enemies hit harder and take more punishment. Loot is scarcer.
• +50% enemy HP & ATK • 25% fewer items • Potions heal 25% less • More enemies per floor
LEGEND BRUTAL
Only the reckless or the desperate choose this path. Death comes swift.
• +100% enemy HP & ATK • 50% fewer items • Potions heal 50% less • Maximum enemies • Enemies are faster

🎓 BEFORE YOU DELVE

🕹️Move with arrow keys / WASD or the D-pad below.
⚔️Walk into an enemy to attack it — there's no separate attack button.
🎒Items are picked up automatically. Use potions from your Inventory.
❤️Watch your HP bar (top-left) — if it hits 0, that run is over.
🐀Floor 1 is a training ground — just a couple of rats to warm up on.
